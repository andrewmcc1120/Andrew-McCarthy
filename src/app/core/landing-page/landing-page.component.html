transend™ is primarily a business to business e-commerce platform, owned by Transtar Industries. Launched in October of 2018, its primary function is to facilitate the sales of transmission parts at a wholesale scale. transend™ provides vehicle search options like VIN, lisence plate, and year/make/model to then give the user a curated matching list of available products for that vehicle. Administrative tools are also provided for the user, such as: Invoice search and pay, order history, and account user control. The front end was created in Angular, and the API's are in C#.

My role in this application was the creation and support of front end features, as well as creating API calls as they were needed. Other work involved the creation and support of an in-house administrative app to support public facing app.
